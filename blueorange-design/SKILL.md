# Blue Orange Digital Design System

Use this alongside the Anthropic frontend-design plugin to ensure all Blue Orange products share a consistent, modern, professional design language.

## Brand Design Language

Reference the live sites for the definitive source:
- **Main site:** https://blueorange.digital
- **AIRQ landing page:** https://blueorange.digital/pages/airq/
- **Blueprint app:** https://blueprint.blueorange.digital

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary Navy/Dark:** Deep navy blue — used for hero sections, headers, footer, primary backgrounds
- **Accent Orange:** Warm orange — used for CTAs, highlights, active states, the "orange" in Blue Orange
- **Light Backgrounds:** Off-white/warm cream — used for content sections, alternating with navy
- **Text on Dark:** White, with orange accent text for emphasis keywords
- **Text on Light:** Dark navy/charcoal, clean and high contrast
- **Gradient accents:** Subtle orange-to-navy transitions in hero areas

### Typography
- Clean, modern sans-serif (not generic — distinctive but professional)
- Large, bold headlines with mixed weight (key words in a lighter or accent color)
- Body text well-spaced and readable
- Metrics/stats displayed prominently with large numbers and small labels

### Layout Patterns
- **Hero sections:** Full-width dark navy with large bold text, accent words in orange/italic, CTA button in orange
- **Stats bar:** Key metrics displayed horizontally with large numbers (e.g., "~2 minutes", "10 dimensions", "50+")
- **Alternating sections:** Dark navy backgrounds alternate with light cream sections
- **Card layouts:** Clean white cards with subtle shadows on light backgrounds
- **Section structure:** Clear vertical rhythm with generous whitespace
- **Navigation:** Top nav bar with Blue Orange logo (left), nav items (center), CTA button (right, orange)
- **Footer:** Dark navy with organized link columns

### UI Components
- **Buttons:** Rounded corners, orange fill for primary CTA, outlined for secondary
- **Cards:** Clean, minimal, with subtle hover effects
- **Forms:** Clean inputs with clear labels
- **Status indicators:** Color-coded, clear at a glance
- **Navigation:** Minimal, functional, not cluttered

### Design Principles
1. **Professional but modern** — enterprise-grade credibility, not startup playful
2. **Clean and spacious** — generous whitespace, not cramped
3. **Data-forward** — metrics and stats are first-class visual elements
4. **Dark + light contrast** — navy sections feel premium, cream sections feel approachable
5. **Orange as accent only** — never overwhelming, always directing attention
6. **Confidence without flash** — the design says "we know what we're doing" without being loud

### What to Avoid
- Generic AI/tech aesthetics (gradients everywhere, floating particles)
- Overly playful or startup-y design
- Cluttered layouts or tiny text
- Stock photo heavy designs
- Generic sans-serif fonts (Inter, system-ui defaults)

## Application to Product UIs (Roster, Blueprint, etc.)

Product UIs (authenticated apps) should feel like a natural extension of the marketing site:
- Same color palette and typography
- Navigation includes the Blue Orange logo
- Darker sidebar or header for admin interfaces
- Light content areas for readability
- Orange for primary actions and active states
- Cards and tables follow the clean, spacious pattern
- Status badges use the color palette (orange for attention, green for good, red for issues)

## How to Use This Skill

When the frontend-design plugin is active, reference this skill for brand-specific decisions:
- "Use the Blue Orange design system colors and layout patterns"
- "Follow the AIRQ/Blueprint page structure for the hero section"
- "Match the Blue Orange card and button styles"

This ensures the frontend-design plugin creates distinctive, production-grade design that also stays on-brand for Blue Orange.
